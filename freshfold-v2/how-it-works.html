How It Works

From your door back to your door in 5 simple steps — all within 24 hours.

1

📅 Schedule a Pickup

Message us on WhatsApp at 0333-3333333 or fill out our order form. Tell us your name, address, number of clothes, and your preferred pickup time. We'll confirm your slot within minutes.

2

🚚 We Pick Up Your Clothes

Our rider arrives at your hostel, home, or office at the confirmed time. Simply hand over your clothes in a bag — no special packaging needed. You'll receive a WhatsApp confirmation with your unique Order ID for tracking.

3

🧼 We Wash & Iron with Care

Each item is tagged with your order ID and processed with professional-grade steam irons. For wash + iron orders, we use the correct wash cycle and temperature for each fabric type. No burns, no damage — guaranteed.

4

📦 Neatly Packed

Once processed, your clothes are folded or hung and packed in clean, branded FreshFold bags. Shirts are hung to avoid re-wrinkling during transit. Shalwar kameez and delicate items are individually folded and layered.

✓

🏠 Delivered to Your Door

Your fresh clothes are returned to your location within 24 hours (standard) or 12 hours (express). You'll receive a WhatsApp notification before the rider arrives. Payment is collected at delivery — cash, Easypaisa, or JazzCash.

FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you cover?

We currently serve university hostels, offices, and residential areas across Rawalpindi and Islamabad. Message us to confirm your specific location.

What if a garment gets damaged?

We handle every garment with care. In the rare event of damage, we will resolve it professionally and responsibly — your trust is our priority.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum — even a single shirt is fine. But bulk orders (10+ items) qualify for a 10% discount, so it pays to send more at once.

How do I pay?

Cash on delivery, Easypaisa, or JazzCash. No advance payment required for first-time customers.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Use your Order ID on our Track Order page to see real-time status from pickup to delivery.
