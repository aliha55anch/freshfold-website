About FreshFold

A student-born startup solving a real everyday problem — one clean shirt at a time.

Our Story

Born in a Hostel Room

It started with a simple frustration. Piles of unironed clothes, limited time, no proper facilities, and the constant stress of looking presentable for class and presentations.

FreshFold was built to solve that. A reliable, affordable, doorstep laundry and ironing service designed specifically for people whose time is too valuable to spend at an ironing board.

We started with a single steam iron and a WhatsApp number. Today we serve hundreds of customers across Rawalpindi — and we're just getting started.
